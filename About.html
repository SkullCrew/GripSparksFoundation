About Us


Mid-Day Meal

We follow the principles of transparency and accountability in all our work. Moreover, this scheme has a positive impact on the overall health of children. It helps prevent malnutrition, which can lead to stunted growth and various health complications. Proper nutrition improves their immune system, enhances cognitive abilities, and contributes to better academic performance.This scheme also has broader benefits for the community. It creates employment opportunities for cooks and helpers involved in meal preparation and distribution. It supports local farmers and promotes the consumption of locally sourced ingredients, contributing to the growth of the agricultural sector.


Free Uniform

We provide free uniforms to all the students in our school.Under this scheme, eligible students receive a set of school uniforms, typically consisting of shirts, trousers/skirts, and sometimes additional items like ties, belts, or shoes. The uniforms are designed to meet the school's dress code requirements and are provided to students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.The Free Uniform Scheme serves several purposes. Firstly, it helps reduce the financial burden on parents or guardians who may struggle to afford school uniforms. By providing free uniforms, the scheme ensures that every child has equal access to education, irrespective of their financial situation.
